1915: The Legends Of Gurkhas (2022)

Venturing into the harrowing depths of World War I's Battle of Loos, '1915 Legends of the Gurkhas' portrays the remarkable journey of Rifleman Kulbir Thapa, a tale of bravery, sacrifice, and unity. Director Pradeep Shahi delicately captures the essence of humanity amidst the chaos of war, as Thapa's selflessness transcends boundaries, earning him the esteemed Victoria Cross. This ground-breaking cinematic masterpiece not only honours the valour of the Gurkha soldiers but also serves as a beacon of hope, supported by a heartfelt initiative for charity and crowdfunding. A poignant tribute to unsung heroes, '1915 Legend of the Gurkhas' leaves an indelible mark on cinema history.

Antim Sanskar : The Last Ritual (2023)

Antim Sanskar: The Last Ritual plunges viewers into a gripping psychological thriller, where the vanishing of acclaimed filmmaker Daya Lama sets the stage for a captivating mystery. As the determined young DSP Rakshya delves into the case, her own demons come to light, adding layers of intrigue. Simultaneously, Daya's son, Dhiraj, embarks on a secretive trek to the Himalayas, his intentions veiled by a mysterious blue bag. Directorial finesse intertwines with spellbinding performances, crafting an atmospheric journey that crescendos with a jaw-dropping revelation amidst the lofty peaks, leaving audiences on the edge of their seats till the very end.
